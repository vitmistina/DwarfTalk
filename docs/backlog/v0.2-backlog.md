# Fortress Souls v0.2 Backlog

Status: Complete
Product specification: `docs/specs/fortress-souls-v0.2.spec.md`  
Tool contract: `docs/specs/perception-tools-v0.2.md`  
Prompt contract: `docs/specs/prompt-contract-v0.2.md`

This backlog delivers perception as small vertical slices. A mini-spec may
refine one item for execution, but it does not supersede the product spec,
accepted ADRs, or tool contract. No item may add mutation, generic DFHack
execution, persistent memory, or autonomous background behavior.

## Observable Outcome

A player asks the selected dwarf about current fortress state. The dwarf may
consult a bounded read-only capability, answer from its validated observation,
and expose a safe receipt identifying the capability used.
